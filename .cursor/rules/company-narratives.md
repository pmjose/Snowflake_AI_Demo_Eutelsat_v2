# Core Company Narratives

Reference: `DATA/companies.csv` (story field)

## 8 Core Companies with Full Story Arcs:

### 1. **SNOW (Snowflake Inc.)** - Market Leader
- **Revenue**: $900M (29% YoY growth)
- **Key Event**: Downgraded to SELL (Sept 19, 2024) due to NRNT threat, recovered to HOLD (Nov 20, 2024)
- **Stock**: -3% during crisis, +6.6% recovery by Nov 29
- **Competes with**: ICBG, QRYQ
- **Partners with**: DFLX, STRM, VLTA, CTLG
- **Message**: Enterprise infrastructure beats consumer hype

### 2. **NRNT (Neuro-Nectar Corporation)** - The Spectacular Failure
- **Revenue**: $142M (487% growth) at peak (Aug 30, 2024)
- **Key Event**: Entered administration Nov 15-20, 2024 due to "severe gastric distress"
- **Stock**: Crashed -90.4% from $133.75 to $12.79, DELISTED Nov 20
- **Timeline**: 62 days from peak hype to extinction
- **Product**: AI-powered cognitive enhancement ice cream (failed)

### 3. **ICBG (ICBG Data Systems)** - Open Lakehouse Alternative
- **Revenue**: $87M (156% growth)
- **Position**: Apache Iceberg-based open lakehouse vs SNOW's proprietary approach
- **Differentiation**: Customer data ownership, no vendor lock-in

### 4. **QRYQ (Querybase Technologies)** - Aggressive Challenger
- **Revenue**: $94M (287% growth), $3B valuation
- **Claims**: 2x better price-performance, 37% win rate vs SNOW
- **Position**: Managed service with open formats (best of both worlds)

### 5. **DFLX (DataFlex Analytics)** - Switzerland BI Platform
- **Revenue**: $67M (143% growth), first positive cash flow
- **Position**: Works across SNOW, QRYQ, ICBG - platform agnostic BI
- **Average Contract Value**: $89K

### 6. **STRM (StreamPipe Systems)** - Real-Time Data Integration
- **Revenue**: $51M (198% growth)
- **Position**: 300+ connectors, source and destination agnostic
- **CEO Quote**: "Circulatory system of the modern data stack"
- **Average Contract Value**: $142K

### 7. **VLTA (Voltaic AI)** - AI-First Platform
- **Revenue**: $73M (312% growth - highest)
- **Position**: Production AI infrastructure (feature stores, MLOps, model serving)
- **Average Contract Value**: $512K (highest)
- **R&D**: $34M/quarter

### 8. **CTLG (CatalogX)** - Governance Layer
- **Revenue**: $48M (176% growth), first positive EBITDA
- **Position**: Cross-platform governance for SNOW, QRYQ, ICBG, STRM, VLTA, DFLX
- **Critical for**: GDPR, CCPA, AI regulation compliance
- **Economics**: 30-40% less than bundled governance

## Bottom 3 Performers (Limited Investor Materials):

- **Status**: Growth stage company investing heavily in customer acquisition

- **Key Metrics**: 890 customers, 125% NRR, -10% operating margin, -$8M FCF

- **Strengths**: Clear vertical leader, strong NRR, approaching profitability
- **Status**: Best of bottom 3, improving unit economics

- **Rating**: OVERWEIGHT (strong fundamentals, improving margins)

### 11. **MKTG (Marketing Analytics)** - Challenged Performer
- **Revenue**: $50M (156% YoY growth) - Q3 FY2025
- **Key Metrics**: 620 customers, 105% NRR (weakest), -40% operating margin, -$16M FCF
