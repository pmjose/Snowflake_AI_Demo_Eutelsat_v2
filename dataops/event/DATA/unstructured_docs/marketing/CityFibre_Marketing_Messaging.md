# Eutelsat Marketing Messaging (Demo)

## Core Value Props
- Multi-orbit performance: resilient GEO fleet plus low-latency OneWeb LEO constellation for always-on connectivity.
- Broadcast leadership: 6,400+ TV channels and 1B viewers served daily across 40+ years of trusted operations (Eutelsat website).
- Tailored solutions by vertical: Broadcast & Video, Aviation, Maritime, Enterprise, Government, Telecom backhaul, and community broadband.
- Reliability & reach: pole-to-pole LEO plus high-throughput GEO for guaranteed coverage on land, at sea, and in the air.

## Priority Campaign Themes (2025)
- **Broadcast & Video**: promote video neighbourhoods, HD/UHD distribution, and managed platforms.
- **Aviation & Maritime**: inflight connectivity and high-speed sea connectivity with seamless GEO/LEO handover.
- **Enterprise & Backhaul**: rural/remote connectivity, edge backhaul, and “Backhaul on Wheels” for rapid restoration.
- **Government & Defence**: secure, resilient links with assured SLAs, disaster recovery, and humanitarian connectivity.
- **Sustainability & ESG**: responsible space, digital inclusion, and earth/space environment commitments.

## Proof Points (from site + provided reports)
- First GEO–LEO satellite network; 40+ years delivering for customers.
- 6,400+ TV channels and ~1B viewers worldwide; strong video neighbourhood footprint.
- Multi-orbit coverage and low-latency services supporting mobility and mission-critical use cases.

## Tone & Guidelines
- Lead with reliability + coverage; emphasise multi-orbit advantage and industry depth.
- Use verified numbers from investor presentations and sustainability statements; mark demo assumptions clearly.
- Keep enterprise/government tone; avoid retail positioning; highlight SLAs and resiliency.
