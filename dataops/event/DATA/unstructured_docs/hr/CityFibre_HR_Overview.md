# Eutelsat HR Overview (Demo)

## Workforce Focus
- Roles: satellite operations, ground gateway engineering, network planning, NOC/SOC, partner success, sales, marketing, finance, HR, product and service design.
- Skills: RF/spectrum, GEO & LEO operations, ground infrastructure design, mobility/backhaul integration, broadcast platform operations, security and compliance.

## Policies (high-level demo summary)
- Safety: ground site access controls, RF safety, and flightline/port safety standards for mobility installs; required PPE and contractor compliance.
- Training: onboarding for satellite/ground operations, partner support tooling, and data handling; refreshers on security and change management.
- Performance: objectives tied to availability/SLA, throughput, partner satisfaction (NPS/CSAT), and activation lead times.
- Flex: hybrid for corporate roles; mission-critical NOC/engineering roles follow shift/on-site coverage; travel as needed for gateways and mobility installs.

## Diversity & Inclusion
- Equal opportunity hiring; inclusive language in postings.
- Encourage early-career/apprenticeship programmes across engineering and operations; support mobility for global ground sites.
